⬥ 12-FACTOR COMPOSITE MODEL ›

1 · Handedness + Pitch Mix

Platoon splits (L/R): OPS & ISO vs pitcher hand. FB% pitchers vs barrel% batters. Chase rate — low chasers see more FBs in zone. Batter performance vs primary pitch type.

2 · Park + Altitude + Humidor

Statcast HR park factor (100=avg). Coors Field: 150 PF + altitude drag reduction. Humidor status. Stadium dimensions and wall height. Microclimate wind tunnels.

3 · Full Weather Stack

Wind speed + direction vector vs park orientation. Barometric pressure — low = less air resistance. Humidity — dry air reduces drag. Temp bonus +2.5ft/10°F above 70°F. Roof status. Time-of-day air density.

4 · Batter Analytics

HR/game (21-day rolling, 60% weight vs 40% season). Barrel% (EV + 8–32° launch). Hard Hit Rate ≥95mph. xSLG, xwOBA, ISO. Sweet spot %. Chase rate. Whiff rate. Pull rate to favorable wall.

5 · Pitcher Vulnerabilities

ERA + HR/9 vs league avg. Hard contact allowed. Fly ball % (high = HR risk). FB velocity sub-91mph flag. Fatigue: ≤3 days rest OR ≥100 pitches last outing = velocity/command drop.

6 · Rolling Averages

15–30 day rolling windows. Pitchers: last 10 starts. Hitters: last 20 games. Season stats weighted 40%, recent form 60%. Platoon splits min 50 PA sample.

7 · ML Model

Binary: HR=1, no HR=0. Logistic regression baseline AUC ~0.74. XGBoost/LightGBM on Statcast tabular data: AUC ~0.83–0.87. Features: barrel%, xSLG, pitcher xBA, park factor, wind, handedness, chase rate, pressure, temp.

8 · Line Movement

Opening vs current HR prop line shift. Sharp signal: line moving toward yes = market confidence. Public vs sharp split via bet% vs handle%. +3 composite bonus for lines moved toward player.

9 · Umpire Tendencies

HP ump strike zone expansion/contraction. Liberal zones → pitcher goes off-speed early. Tight zones → more FBs in zone for batter. ±2 composite adjustment per ump favor score.

10 · Travel + Fatigue

Cross-timezone travel: East→West vs West→East jet lag. Consecutive games played. Days since last off day. Road team 3-city trip Day 6+. Circadian misalignment → reduced bat speed.

11 · Spray Angle + Pull

Pull% to short porch or favorable wall distance. Hard-hit pull% amplified at CBP, Yankee Stadium. Spray angle vs stadium dimensions. Outfield positioning shifts and OAA.

12 · Score Calibration

0–99 composite. Power Base 28pts, OPS/SLG 22pts, Barrel+HH 13pts, Pitcher Vuln 18pts, Park+Wind+Weather 15pts, Platoon 8pts, Line Move 3pts. S≥80, A≥65, B≥50, C≥35.

# ── PYBASEBALL: Rolling barrel rate + Statcast ─────────────────────── from pybaseball import statcast_batter import pandas as pd df = statcast_batter('2026-03-28','2026-06-02', player_id=592450) df['hr'] = (df['events']=='home_run').astype(int) df['game_date'] = pd.to_datetime(df['game_date']) df = df.sort_values('game_date') df['barrel_r21'] = df['barrel'].rolling(21,min_periods=5).mean() df['hardHit_r21'] = (df['launch_speed']>=95).rolling(21,min_periods=5).mean()*100 df['sweet_r21'] = df['launch_angle'].between(8,32).rolling(21,min_periods=5).mean()*100 # ── MLB STATS API: Today's schedule ────────────────────────────────── import requests, datetime today = datetime.date.today().strftime('%Y-%m-%d') r = requests.get(f'https://statsapi.mlb.com/api/v1/schedule', params={'sportId':1,'date':today,'hydrate':'probablePitcher(note),team,venue,weather'}) games = r.json()['dates'][0]['games'] for g in games: away = g['teams']['away']['team']['name'] home = g['teams']['home']['team']['name'] ap = g['teams']['away'].get('probablePitcher',{}).get('fullName','TBD') hp = g['teams']['home'].get('probablePitcher',{}).get('fullName','TBD') print(f"{away} @ {home} | {ap} vs {hp}") # ── OPEN-METEO: Weather at Citizens Bank Park ──────────────────────── r = requests.get("https://api.open-meteo.com/v1/forecast", params={ "latitude":39.9055,"longitude":-75.1665, "hourly":"windspeed_10m,winddirection_10m,temperature_2m,relativehumidity_2m,surface_pressure", "timezone":"America/New_York","forecast_days":1}) wx = r.json()['hourly'] game_hr = 16 print(f"Wind: {wx['windspeed_10m'][game_hr]*0.621:.1f}mph @ {wx['winddirection_10m'][game_hr]}°") print(f"Temp: {wx['temperature_2m'][game_hr]*9/5+32:.0f}°F | Pressure: {wx['surface_pressure'][game_hr]:.0f}hPa") # ── XGBOOST: HR classification ──────────────────────────────────────── import xgboost as xgb from sklearn.model_selection import train_test_split from sklearn.metrics import roc_auc_score features = ['barrel_r21','hardHit_r21','sweet_r21', 'estimated_slugging_using_speedangle','launch_speed'] X = df[features].dropna() y = df.loc[X.index,'hr'] X_tr,X_te,y_tr,y_te = train_test_split(X,y,test_size=.2,stratify=y,random_state=42) m = xgb.XGBClassifier(n_estimators=400,max_depth=4,learning_rate=.04, scale_pos_weight=len(y[y==0])/len(y[y==1]),eval_metric='auc',random_state=42) m.fit(X_tr,y_tr,eval_set=[(X_te,y_te)],early_stopping_rounds=25,verbose=False) print(f"XGBoost AUC: {roc_auc_score(y_te,m.predict_proba(X_te)[:,1]):.3f}") # expected: 0.83 – 0.87
